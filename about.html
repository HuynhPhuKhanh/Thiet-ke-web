Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise rountine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squad and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and than forward again quickly.
Plank
Plank build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can(as though you are climbing a mountain).
